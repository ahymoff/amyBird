Notes

As I was getting an understanding of the crime of cutting up money, I discovered online the money collage art of Mark Wagner. I was both thrilled and devastated to find it. Thrilled because it is beautiful and technically and edgy and interesting. Devastated because his money collages and his blogs are brilliant in ways I knew I could never be brilliant and I had seen them before I had even seen or started my own work. Why bother, I thought.
